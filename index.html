Our Story

Cloud Zone™ Vape Shop was founded in 2016 by Dave Luna, Garry Jr. and Jairo De Guzman. Unique in the industry, Cloud Zone™ is driven by the desire to help people quit smoking by offering only top quality, hand-picked vape products and accessories, educational articles, and an exceptional customer service experience. Each and every product is thoroughly tested by our staff to ensure it meets, or exceeds, our expectations. If a product fails to impress us, we won't sell it. Simple. We back this up with the only 30 day no-hassle warranty in the industry. Over the years, Cloud Zone™ has grown from an upstart vape shop to three retail locations, with an online partnership to deliver the same service anywhere in Canada. We have partnered to maintain, and staff, our own warehouse in Dieppe, New Brunswick, where each online order ships from. This location allows us fast shipping in Atlantic Canada with local next day delivery services. Whether you're looking to quit smoking, or for the best gear in vape and 420, Cloud Zone™ has what you're looking for.
